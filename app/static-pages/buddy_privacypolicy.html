Buddy - Android Privacy Policy

This privacy policy is designed to help you understand how data may be collected from your interactions with the Android port of the game "Buddy", hosted on Google Play.

What data will be collected?

No data is collected under any circumstances from playing the game. There is no means for the game to collect data through play, it does not communicate with anything that requires transferral of data. It is an entirely offline experience.

Contact information may be obtained and retained when you, the user, reach out to me at my support email.

This game uses Unity Ads for advertising, which may use and collect anonymous data about your interests to customize advertising. Interest and location data may be linked to your device, but not your identity. Please check the link below to see Unity's Privacy Policy and opt-out capabilities.
Unity Privacy Policy - https://unity.com/legal/privacy-policy

Why will this data be collected?

Contact information will be collected and utilised for the following:
- To support you as the user with the game
- For further contact regarding support or game issues

How can I delete my advertising data?

You can request for your advertising data collected by Unity Ads to be deleted by getting in touch with Unity at DPO@unity3d.com

Safeguarding

Any information that is collected is generally not retained for any longer than is necessary. When data is retained, it will be kept confidential. I will not share any of your data with third parties or sell your data. Data may be disclosed if required by law.

Contact

If you wish to get in contact with me for support purposes, have any questions, or want to opt-out of having contact information retained, you can contact me at leif.unity3dbusiness@gmail.com
