Cyberblaze

Defend the data stream!

Cyberblaze is a wave defense shooter in which you are left to fend for yourself.
Fighting off swarms off enemies with a varied arsenal.

You must use everything at your disposal to defend yourself.
Featuring a varied roster of enemies, weaponry and a simplistic, blocky art style.
The more you play the game, the more powerful you become thanks to a simple
progression system, with levelling, XP gain, game modifiers, skills and more!
