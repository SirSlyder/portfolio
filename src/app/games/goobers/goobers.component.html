Goobers

Stronger together!

Goobers is a 2D puzzle platformer developed for the Brackeys 2021.1 Jam by myself and Esther Holt.
Developed over the course of 7 days, with the theme being "Stronger Together".
It ranked 229th overall, out of 1831 entries.

You must rescue your little Goober pals and escape!
You must navigate a variety of levels, and use 3 unique goober types
to your advantage and reach the exit. But you must plan ahead or else
your newfound size will backfire on you.
Featuring a simple, cutesy pixelated style and some bouncy, slimey animation.
