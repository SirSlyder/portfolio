Slaughter Lands

How long can you survive?

Slaughter Lands is a Call Of Duty Zombies inspired game, in which the player must survive against hordes of the undead.

This game holds a special (if janky) place in my heart, as it is
the first Unity game I ever made.
It was in development since 2015, when I was 13.
And has gone over many, MANY iterations.
Sadly the game is no longer in development, due to countless issues
arising from Unity deprecating JavaScript and other asset troubles
as the engine was updated.
