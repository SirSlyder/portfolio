Buddy

Can you escape?

Buddy is a short point and click horror game in which you must escape from a room controlled by the eponymous Buddy.
Will you make out before he catches you?

You must use your wits and the tools available to you to escape from Buddy's room.
Or die trying.
The game is all hand-drawn artwork, created by myself in Paint Tool SAI
There are many endings to obtain, each with unique outcomes.
Some good. Some bad.

Enter, if you dare.
